Our Story

School of Data and AI was founded in Kanpur by Arpit Tiwari, a Senior Data Scientist with 5+ years of experience in Data Science and Generative AI. After mentoring 50+ students and seeing the gap between industry needs and what traditional education offered, he started SDAI with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to practical, career-changing skills in Data and AI.

We began with free weekly masterclasses to help people understand what Data and AI mean in the real job market. The response showed a real hunger for skills that lead to jobs — not just certificates. So we built structured tracks: from “AI for Everyone” to deep-dive programs in Applied AI, Python, and Data Analytics.

Today, SDAI is rooted in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, with learners and ambition that reach far beyond. We are here to make Data and AI skills accessible, practical, and career-transforming — starting from Kanpur, reaching the world.

Our mission: To make Data and AI skills accessible, practical, and career-transforming — starting from Kanpur, reaching the world.
